Our Story

From Homemaker
to Fashion Designer

Nishana Couture began an inspiring journey in 2019 — born from a homemaker's passion, dedication, and an eye for unique styles. What started as a personal love for fashion has grown into a beloved boutique brand that brings beauty and comfort to every outfit.

With a deep understanding of both modern trends and timeless traditions, Nishana crafts each piece to reflect the personality of the wearer. Every stitch tells a story of craftsmanship, every fabric chosen with care.

Located in the heart of Chennai, our boutique reflects the essence of creativity and quality. We specialize in kids' fashion, ladies' wear, and the unique joy of perfectly matched Mom & Kid combos.
